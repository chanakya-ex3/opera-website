About Us


Today's demanding statutory compliance, information updates, and timely posting of reports and detailed record-keeping and required reporting is necessary to improve the standard of regulatory compliance of your organization. Here's a little in today's workplace that demands more time and, failing to do so, there's little that could hit your bottom line fines or negligence lawsuits.

Opera Project Management Consultants Pvt Limited (A Group of Omega School of industrial Management) is a team of dedicated Statutory consulting professionals, whose practice is the structure and implementation of statutory compliance, company law matters including maintenance of legal forms records, as required by the various statutory bodies of State Central. Every member of our advisors staff not only holds highly technical degrees but they have practical “in-field experience. We bring a reality of what works and what does not to every situation. As consultants, we think strategically to accomplish industrial statutory compliance within the big picture of your business.



Our Vision

“We as statutory consultancy professionals, the OPMC would steer and assist our customers to implement the highest standard of industrial statutory and legal compliances on and off at their work premises/places effectively”.

Create awarenesss on industrial statutory requirement through periodic audit & implementation, training event, including the amendments/notifications etc.on the highest standards of statutory & frame work at affordable professional charges, offer quality service and ensure the customer satisfaction.
